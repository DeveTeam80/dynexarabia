About Us

About Us

We Are DYNEX Arabia

We are a 100% Saudi-owned LSTK-EPC contractor. We take pride in being part of Al-Awael Group, which is owned by the Al-Rashid Group (RAR). We specialize in constructing pipelines and ancillary facilities for Saudi Arabia's vital O&G, energy and water sectors.

As a Category 1+ contractor with Saudi Aramco, we are proud to execute Super Mega Projects that contribute directly to the Kingdom’s sustainable development.

DYNEX Arabia Story

Since inception, we’ve delivered end-to-end EPC solutions in O&G and water, powered by local expertise, global standards, and a clear national vision. As part of Saudi Arabia’s vision, DYNEX aims to capture 44 million tons of CO₂ annually by 2035.

Join us on a journey of innovation, safety, and excellence, where we empower Saudi talent and engineer solutions built to last.
DYNEX Arabia. Bridging a Sustainable Future.

OUR PURPOSE

IT’S NOT JUST WHAT WE BUILD, BUT THE FUTURE WE SHAPE

OUR GOAL: TO BE THE LEADING PIPELINE AND ENERGY SOLUTIONS PROVIDER IN THE MENA REGION

We are committed to shaping a safer, brighter, and more sustainable tomorrow. Our purpose drives us to expand into new markets, deliver cutting-edge contracting solutions, and empower Saudi talent to lead the region’s progress. Our Core Values

Quality & Safety

We are committed to meeting high international standards. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification reflects our focus on quality. We prioritize health, safety, and environmental management to ensure zero incidents on every project.

Ethics & Integrity

We conduct our business transparently, respectfully, and strictly adhere to legal and ethical standards. This commitment builds trust-based relationships with clients, partners, and employees, reinforcing our reputation as a dependable and principled industry leader.

What Sets Us Apart

DYNEX Arabia stands out with on-time, high-quality project delivery backed by deep technical expertise and strong financials. This makes us a preferred, future-ready partner and keeps us ahead in the marketspace.
