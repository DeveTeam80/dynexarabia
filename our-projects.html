Khursaniyah Upstream Gas Pipelines

  • Construction of 58 KM of pipeline
  • Two large Gas Gathering Manifold plants
  • Installation of SCADA and Control Systems
  • Installation of Leak Detection and Corrosion Monitoring systems

Three Northern Area Pipelines

  • Construction of 108 KM of pipeline.
  • Eight Mainline Valve Stations with associated facilities.
  • Installation of two Scraper Trap facilities.

Pipeline Rehabilitation 2016

  • Construction of 173 KM of pipeline.
  • Installation of two Jump-overs and four scraper traps.
  • Installation of 12 valve stations.

Zuluf AH Development- Downstream Pipelines

  • Construction of 207 KM of pipeline.
  • Installation of 6 Surge Relief Skids at 4 plants and the main pipeline.
  • Installation of 6 scrapers and 3 Mainline Isolation Valve stations.
  • Performance of 9 Hot Taps.

Reroute Khursaniyah Ras-Tanura Pipelines

  • Construction of 355 KM of pipeline.
  • Full and partial replacement of 5 Saudi Aramco and 5 third party pipelines.
  • Installation of scraper traps, valve stations and fiber optic cables.
  • More than 100 Hot Taps to be completed.

Jafurah Upstream Pipelines-Phase II

  • Construction of 380 KM of pipeline.
  • Procurement of all Metallic and Nonmetallic pipes.
  • Construction of backbone and lateral pipelines of 3 Gas.
  • Compression Facilities and Jafurah Gas Plant along with associated Scraper Trap Stations and Valve Stations.

Dammam Field Development – Phase II

  • Construction of 50 KM of pipeline.
  • Adding 50 million barrel of crude oil per day.

Accelerated Carbon Capture and Sequestration – Pipeline and Sink Facilities (Package 2)

  • Installation of 5 Mainline Isolation Valve Stations.
  • Custody Meters and Filtration for each of the 9 Injection Wells.
  • Installation of 116 KM of Overhead Power Lines.
